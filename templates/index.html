Symptoms Of Covid19

DRY COUGH

A cough not accompanied by phlegm production.

FEVER

A fever is a temporary increase in your body temperature.

TIREDNESS

The state of wishing for sleep or rest; weariness.

SORE THROAT

A sore throat is a throat's pain that often worsens when you swallow.

DIARRHOEA

When you have diarrhea, your stool will be loose and watery.

HEADACHE

Headache is defined as pain arising from the head or upper neck of the body.

CHESTPAIN

This is an inflammation or irritation of the lining of the lungs and chest.

SHORTNESS OF BREATH

Shortness of breath is described as an intense tightening in the chest or difficulty breathing.
